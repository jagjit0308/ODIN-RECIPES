Lasagna Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken

Description

This recipe originated from the country of India.

Ingredients

  • Chicken in 8 pieces
  • Corns
  • Butter
  • Masalas and Cream

Preparation Method

  1. Pour butter in a pan and heat it
  2. Fry the chicken in a pan till it color changes to brown
  3. Add some water and cook it till the chicken leaves its oil.
  4. Cook till it get golden brown color
  5. Add Masalas and Cream to Garnish
