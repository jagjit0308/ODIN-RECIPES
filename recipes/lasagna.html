Lasagna Butter Chicken

Lasagna

Description

This recipe originated from the country of Japan.

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Corns
  • Cheese
  • Mint Leaves

Preparation Method

  1. Mix some water in wheat Flour and make a dough
  2. Flat the dough in round shape and cook in a pan
  3. After cooking add corns and cheese over it.
  4. Cook till it get golden brown color
  5. Garnish the Dish with mint leaves
